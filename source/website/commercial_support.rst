==================
Commercial support
==================

Do you plan to use QGIS in your company or organization, but worry about whether there is commercial support available? Here is an alphabetical list, divided in categories, of support contractors.

.. note:: we take responsibility for the accuracy, reliability or completeness of the provided information for **Core contributors** only.

Core contributors
------------------

.. image:: images/3liz.png
   :height: 80 px
   :align: left

`3liz <http://www.3liz.com/>`_ (based in France) offers a full range of services around QGIS, to escort all software users, from beginners to the insiders. Depending on your needs, 3Liz gives training sessions to QGIS, provides the Web mapping portal LizMap - based on QGIS Desktop & QGIS Serveur - which allows easy publication of data on the Web, and also proposes support, development and consulting on QGIS.
